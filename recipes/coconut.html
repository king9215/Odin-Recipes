Nigerian coconut Rice

Description

Nigerian Coconut Rice is one of my favorite rice recipes,if you’re wondering if you should buy some coconut to make this recipe, you don’t. Just purchase regular coconut milk from your store, and it would do just fine.

For my recipe, I decided to add a variety of peppers,However, if spicy food is not your thing, then you can skip it. But in all honesty, the additional peppers do make this recipe taste BOMB (so I suggest giving it a shot).

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp coconut oil (or any oil you prefer)
  • 1 cup diced red onions
  • ½ tsp ground red pepper
  • ½ tsp curry
  • ½ tsp oregano
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 maggi cubes
  • ⅛ cup crayfish blended
  • 3 habanero peppers (chopped)
  • 1 cup sweet peppers (chopped)
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 ½ cup long grain parboiled rice thoroughly washed

Steps

  1. To a medium-sized pot, heat up your oil on medium heat and throw in your chopped red onions. Fry until the onion redness starts to fade.
  2. Add in your crayfish, peppers, curry, salt, oregano, maggi cubes, habanero peppers and sweet peppers. Stir for about 5 mins
  3. Pour in your coconut milk and stir with the other ingredients
  4. Add in your rice and cook for 45 mins on medium heat
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
