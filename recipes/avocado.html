Avocado Toast and Eggs

Description

Who else loves avocado toast? Because I know for a fact that I do.

Avocado is one fruit that is very rich in fiber. So if you’re wanting a meal that will fill you up for an entire morning, you should consider this recipe.I honestly love it so much and I eat mine with eggs and a sprinkle of salt and pepper, Because avocado on bread without seasoning does have a bland taste. However, it’s completely optional if choose to eat it with eggs.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium avocado
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 2 slices toast bread
  • 1/16 tsp salt
  • 1/16 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Beat eggs and a pinch of salt in a medium size bowl and fry on medium heat until done.
  2. In a small bowl scoop in avocado, salt, pepper and combine
  3. Spread mixture on toasted bread and lay on fried eggs.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
